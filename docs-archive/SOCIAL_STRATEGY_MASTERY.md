## Engagement Hacks

### The "3-Reply" Rule
When someone comments on your post:
1. Reply to first 3 comments immediately
2. Algorithm sees this as engagement signal
3. Post gets 40% more reach

### The "Quote Tweet" Strategy
- Find competitor posts, quote with your counter-point
- "They're right BUT..." format gets 2-3x retweets
- Adds volume without seeming like spam

### The "Curiosity Gap" Formula
- State claim: "RTX 5070 is better than RTX 4090"
- Tease payoff: "3 reasons why..."
- Force click-through (for blog) or full-read (social)

### The "Audience Segmentation" Play
- Generate VIRAL tone for TikTok/Instagram (max engagement)
- Generate PROFESSIONAL tone for Twitter/LinkedIn (credibility)
- Same topic, different platforms get different tones
